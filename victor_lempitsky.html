Scientific interests

Research is about designing computer systems that can extract, organize, and quantify information contained in images of various types and origin, including new machine learning techniques (deep learning in particular) and optimization techniques that are robust and flexible enough to handle and to adapt to the diversity of image data in the modern world.

Current activities in the Laboratory

Victor is supervising and coordinating research activities related to imaging. Victor's group is working on new deep learning-based solutions for a variety of problems in computer vision as well as in image synthesis and image processing.
