Oseledets Ivan is the winner of an award of President of the Russian Federation

February 8, 2019

On Feb. 7, 2019, Russian President Vladimir Putin presented the 2018 Presidential Prizes in Science and Innovation for Young Scientists. One of the five prize-winning researchers is Ivan Oseledets.

Oseledets received the award for “proposing breakthrough computational technology for solving multidimensional problems in physics, chemistry, biology, and data analysis based on tensor expansions.” The researcher laid the foundation for computational tensor methods, a new approach in applied mathematics that enables the efficient processing of large amounts of multidimensional data emerging in applied tasks in physics, chemistry, biology, and data analysis. The proposed approach underlies TT-Toolbox, an open-source software suite developed by Oseledets and used by research groups around the world.
