Scientific interests

Current activities in the Laboratory

Diana is working on neural methods for face image editing. She is looking for principled methods that would allow to combine and improve state-of-the-art in this area. The methods considered by Diana include those based on adversarial training, autoencoding architectures, deep warping techniques, perceptual losses.
