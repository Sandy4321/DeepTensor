Overview

In 2019 Skoltech and Marchuk Institute of Numerical Mathematics, RAS are organizing 5-th International Conference on Matrix Methods in Mathematics and applications (MMMA-2019). Nowadays, matrix methods are in very basement of modern numerical mathematics, computational fluid dynamics, machine learning and many other spheres of science and applications. Novel results in this field of research may have affect on life of extremely broad community of researchers, engineers and commercial companies. The aim of this conference is to present and discuss recent results in development of matrix methods, numerical linear algebra, tensor decompositions in both fundamental and applied scopes. We have invited top active researchers working in corresponding fields of science of Russia and also from Greece, Italy, Scotland, USA and other countries. Each speaker will present original work as well as significant work-in-progress covering novel theories, methods and meaningful applications. This workshop is an opportunity to bring together academic and industrial scientists to discuss the recent advances.

The topics of interest include but not limited to:

  • Matrix factorizations
  • Tensor decompositions
  • Modelling of problems in higher dimensions
  • Tensor networks
  • Application of matrix methods in machine learning
  • Parallel algorithms
